FAQ

What is $GET?

$GET is the rewards token powering Getchain — a decentralized incentive system that lets users earn tokens by participating in the ecosystem and contributing value.

How do I qualify for the $GET airdrop?

You must have participated in eligible campaigns or connected a verified wallet before the snapshot date to be eligible for the airdrop.

Snapshot Dates

Season 1 Airdrop — Snapshot: June 10, 2024, 00:00 UTC
Bounty Blitz Campaign — Snapshot: April 21, 2024, 00:00 UTC
Referral Campaign — Snapshot: July 15, 2024, 00:00 UTC

Do I need to pay gas fees?

Yes, a standard gas fee is required to claim your tokens. If you don’t claim immediately, tokens will remain in your claim pool until you do.

How long do I have to claim?

You have up to 3 months after the vesting period ends to claim your $GET tokens. After that, unclaimed tokens may be reallocated to the community pool.

Why am I not eligible?

Make sure you:

  • Connected the correct wallet at the time of the snapshot
  • Completed required tasks or activity
  • Met all campaign criteria

Can I use a different wallet to claim?

No — the airdrop is locked to the wallet connected to your profile at the time of the snapshot.
